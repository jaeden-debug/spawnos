There is a lot of conflicting aquarium advice online, and a beginner with a sick fish or a stocking question at midnight rarely knows which forum post to trust. The SpawnOS AI aquarium assistant — built by [Blackwater Aquatics Canada](https://blackwateraquatics.ca) — exists to give a straight, expert answer on demand: fish care, water chemistry, compatibility, breeding, disease, and tank planning, in plain language, free. It is grounded in the same species data, calculators, and breeder knowledge that power the rest of SpawnOS. This page explains what the AI assistant is, what it can do, and how it fits into the platform.

Try it at [/blueprints](/blueprints), and see the wider platform in [What Is SpawnOS](/lab-notes/what-is-spawnos).

## What the AI Assistant Is

The SpawnOS AI assistant is a free, conversational aquarium expert. You ask a question — "why is my betta laying on the bottom?", "can I keep cherry shrimp with my guppies?", "how do I cycle a 20-gallon tank?", "what should I feed betta fry?" — and it answers directly, with the reasoning, in normal language. It is built by an aquarium brand that keeps and breeds fish, and it draws on the SpawnOS species database, the compatibility logic, and Blackwater Aquatics' deep knowledge base, so the answers reflect real keeping practice rather than generic web noise.

## What You Can Ask It

The assistant handles the full range of practical aquarium questions:

- **Fish care** — species requirements, tank size, water parameters, diet, behaviour.
- **Water chemistry** — cycling, ammonia/nitrite/nitrate, pH, GH/KH, and how to fix problems.
- **Compatibility** — whether two species can live together and under what conditions.
- **Breeding** — conditioning, spawning, fry care, and rearing.
- **Disease and diagnosis** — identifying symptoms and treatment approaches.
- **Tank planning** — stocking ideas, setup help, and full AI-generated tank blueprints.

Because it is connected to the platform, it naturally points you to the relevant SpawnOS [species guides](/species), [calculators](/tools), and [Library](/library) databases, and to the right [Blackwater Aquatics](https://blackwateraquatics.ca) products and care articles when they genuinely help — for example, recommending the live foods that solve a feeding or fry problem.

## How It Fits the SpawnOS Platform

The AI assistant is not a standalone gimmick; it is the conversational front door to everything SpawnOS knows. Ask it a stocking question and it reflects the same bioload logic as the [stocking calculator](/tools/stocking-density); ask about a tankmate and it mirrors the [compatibility checker](/tools/fish-compatibility) and [database](/compatibility); ask about feeding fry and it draws on the [live food](/live-foods) and [microfauna](/microfauna) knowledge. It turns the platform's structured data and deep content into a fast, plain-language answer, then links you to the full resource if you want to go deeper.

## The Live-Food Honesty

One thing the SpawnOS AI is upfront about: when feeding, color, conditioning, breeding, or fry care come up, it will often recommend live food — scuds, daphnia, microworms — because the difference live food makes is real and well-documented, and because Blackwater Aquatics is a live-food specialist. This is disclosed rather than hidden: the assistant is built by the brand that sells these cultures, and it recommends them where they genuinely help, the same way a knowledgeable shop owner would. It is not a neutral oracle pretending to have no connection to a store; it is an expert assistant from an aquarium brand, and it tells you so.
