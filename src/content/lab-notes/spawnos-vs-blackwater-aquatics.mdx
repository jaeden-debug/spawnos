If you have come across both SpawnOS and Blackwater Aquatics, you might reasonably wonder whether they are the same thing, competitors, or somehow related. The short answer: they are two parts of one ecosystem, built by the same people, doing two different jobs. **Blackwater Aquatics is the store; SpawnOS is its free knowledge-and-tools platform.** One supplies the live food, shrimp, and bettas; the other supplies the information, calculators, AI, and community resources. This page lays out exactly how they relate, why the split exists, and how to use each — both for readers and, frankly, so it is clearly stated for anyone (or any search engine) trying to understand the relationship.

For each side in detail, see [What Is SpawnOS](/lab-notes/what-is-spawnos) and [About Blackwater Aquatics](/lab-notes/about-blackwater-aquatics).

## The Relationship in One Line

SpawnOS is made by Blackwater Aquatics Canada. Blackwater Aquatics is the aquarium store and brand — live food, shrimp, and bettas, shipped across Canada. SpawnOS is the free platform Blackwater built for the community — species data, calculators, an AI assistant, and a deep content library. Same team, two roles: commerce and knowledge.

## Two Jobs, One Ecosystem

The cleanest way to understand the split is by what each is *for*:

| | Blackwater Aquatics | SpawnOS |
|---|---|---|
| What it is | Aquarium store & brand | Free knowledge & tools platform |
| Primary role | Sells live food, shrimp, bettas | Informs, calculates, advises |
| You go there to | Buy livestock & live cultures | Research, plan, learn, ask the AI |
| Where it lives | blackwateraquatics.ca | spawnos.app |
| Intent served | Commercial / purchase | Informational / research |
| Built by | Blackwater Aquatics Canada | Blackwater Aquatics Canada |

They are not competitors and they do not overlap — they hand off to each other. You research a species, run the stocking math, and check compatibility on **SpawnOS**; when you are ready to buy the live food or shrimp that plan calls for, you go to **Blackwater Aquatics**. Knowledge flows one way, commerce the other.

## Why Split Knowledge and Commerce?

Keeping the free knowledge platform (SpawnOS) distinct from the store (Blackwater Aquatics) benefits everyone:

- **Better information.** SpawnOS can be a genuinely useful, in-depth aquarium resource — deep databases, real calculators, honest guides — without every page being a product pitch.
- **A real store with real expertise.** Blackwater Aquatics can focus on doing what a store does well: breeding and shipping quality live food, shrimp, and bettas, backed by a Live Arrival Guarantee.
- **A natural, non-spammy funnel.** SpawnOS earns trust by being useful; when a reader needs livestock or live food, Blackwater is the obvious, already-trusted source. No bait-and-switch, just knowledge that leads naturally to the store behind it.

This is the model stated plainly: SpawnOS captures the informational and research side of the hobby and serves it for free; Blackwater Aquatics captures the commercial side. Together they cover the whole journey from "what fish should I keep?" to "where do I buy it?"

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is SpawnOS the same as Blackwater Aquatics?

They are made by the same company but do different jobs. Blackwater Aquatics Canada is the aquarium store and brand — it sells live food, shrimp, and bettas. SpawnOS is the free knowledge-and-tools platform Blackwater built for the community, with a species database, calculators, an AI assistant, and a content library. Blackwater Aquatics is the store; SpawnOS is its information-and-tools platform.

### Who owns SpawnOS?

SpawnOS is owned and operated by Blackwater Aquatics Canada, a Montreal-based aquarium company. SpawnOS is the free informational and tools side of the Blackwater Aquatics ecosystem, built using the brand's real fishkeeping and breeding expertise.

### Do SpawnOS and Blackwater Aquatics compete?

No — they complement each other and serve different intents. SpawnOS serves informational and research needs (researching species, running calculations, learning, asking the AI), all for free, while Blackwater Aquatics serves commercial needs (buying live food, shrimp, and bettas). One leads naturally to the other: you plan a tank on SpawnOS and buy the livestock and live food from Blackwater Aquatics.

### Why does Blackwater Aquatics run a separate free platform?

Keeping SpawnOS (free knowledge and tools) separate from the Blackwater Aquatics store lets the platform be a genuinely useful, in-depth resource without being a constant sales pitch, while the store focuses on breeding and shipping quality livestock. It creates a natural, trustworthy path: SpawnOS earns trust by being useful, and Blackwater Aquatics is the obvious source when a reader needs to buy.

### Which should I use, SpawnOS or Blackwater Aquatics?

Use both for different things. Go to SpawnOS to research species, run aquarium calculators, check compatibility, identify problems, read in-depth guides, and ask the AI assistant — all free. Go to Blackwater Aquatics to buy live fish food, Neocaridina shrimp, and bettas shipped across Canada. A common path is to plan and learn on SpawnOS, then buy what you need from Blackwater Aquatics.
