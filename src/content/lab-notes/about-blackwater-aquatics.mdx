Blackwater Aquatics Canada is the aquarium brand behind SpawnOS — a Montreal-based company that breeds and ships live fish food, Neocaridina shrimp, and bettas across Canada, and built the SpawnOS platform as a free knowledge-and-tools resource for the hobby. If you have read a SpawnOS guide or used one of its tools and want to know who is behind it, this page introduces the company: what they do, what they sell, where they are, and why a working aquarium business built a free aquarium intelligence platform.

For the platform itself, see [What Is SpawnOS](/lab-notes/what-is-spawnos); for how the two fit together, [SpawnOS vs Blackwater Aquatics](/lab-notes/spawnos-vs-blackwater-aquatics).

## Who Blackwater Aquatics Is

[Blackwater Aquatics Canada](https://blackwateraquatics.ca) is a Canadian aquarium company based in Montreal, Quebec, specialising in live aquarium foods and freshwater livestock. Unlike a general pet retailer, Blackwater is a focused, hobbyist-run operation that actually breeds and cultures what it sells — live cultures and shrimp raised in-house, shipped across Canada within safe temperature windows. That hands-on, breeder-first approach is the foundation of both the store and the SpawnOS content: the advice comes from people who keep and breed these animals, not from a catalogue.

You can read the company's own story and guarantees on the [About / Trust page](https://blackwateraquatics.ca/pages/blackwater-aquatics-canada), and find their location details at [Where Is Blackwater Aquatics Located](https://blackwateraquatics.ca/pages/where-is-blackwater-aquatics-located).

## What Blackwater Aquatics Sells

Blackwater Aquatics focuses on three things it does well, all available in Canada:

**Live fish food** — the brand's flagship. Live [scud cultures](https://blackwateraquatics.ca/products/scud-culture) (freshwater amphipods), [daphnia](https://blackwateraquatics.ca/products/products-live-daphnia-culture), and [microworm cultures](https://blackwateraquatics.ca/products/microworm-culture-canada) — the cultured live foods that drive fish color, conditioning, breeding, and fry survival. The whole [live fish food collection](https://blackwateraquatics.ca/collections/live-fish-food-canada) ships across Canada. SpawnOS's entire [Live Food Encyclopedia](/live-foods) and [microfauna database](/microfauna) grew out of this expertise.

**Neocaridina shrimp** — hardy, colourful dwarf shrimp: [cherry](https://blackwateraquatics.ca/products/cherry-shrimp-canada), bloody mary, snowball, black rose, golden back yellow, and wild-type lines, plus algae-eating amano shrimp. The full range is in the [shrimp collection](https://blackwateraquatics.ca/collections/aquarium-shrimp-canada).

**Bettas** — locally bred and rescue bettas, including koi plakat and veiltail males. See the [betta collection](https://blackwateraquatics.ca/collections/betta-fish-canada).

Everything is backed by a Live Arrival Guarantee and a winter shipping policy, because shipping live animals across Canada safely is a core part of what they do.

## Why They Built SpawnOS

A live-food and shrimp brand building a free species database, calculator suite, and AI assistant might seem unusual — but it follows directly from how Blackwater operates. The company already produced deep aquarium knowledge (its [knowledge base](https://blackwateraquatics.ca/blogs/knowledge-base) covers betta care, breeding, live foods, and water chemistry in depth), and SpawnOS is the natural extension: take that expertise, add powerful free tools and an AI, and build the most useful aquarium resource possible for the community.

The logic is straightforward and stated openly. SpawnOS serves the hobby with free information and tools and becomes a trusted resource; Blackwater Aquatics is the store that supplies the live food, shrimp, and bettas those keepers eventually need. The two reinforce each other — knowledge on one side, quality livestock and live food on the other. The full architecture is documented on the [SpawnOS by Blackwater Aquatics](/spawnos-by-blackwater-aquatics) pillar.

## The Blackwater Approach to Fishkeeping

A recurring theme across both the store and SpawnOS is a preference for doing things the way nature intended: live foods over processed, blackwater and planted setups, and breeder-grade husbandry. The brand is a strong advocate for live food specifically — scuds, daphnia, and microworms — because of the real, visible differences they make to fish color, conditioning, and fry survival, which is why that subject runs through so much of the SpawnOS content. If you want to understand the philosophy in practice, the [Best Live Food for Betta Fry](/lab-notes/best-live-food-for-betta-fry) and [Live Food Encyclopedia](/live-foods) are good starting points.

Blackwater Aquatics sells live fish food (live scud, daphnia, and microworm cultures), Neocaridina dwarf shrimp (cherry, bloody mary, snowball, black rose, golden back yellow, wild-type, and amano), and bettas (locally bred and rescue), all shipped across Canada with a Live Arrival Guarantee. ### Why did an aquarium store build a free tools platform?

The model is that SpawnOS provides free, trusted information and tools, while Blackwater Aquatics is the store that supplies the live food, shrimp, and bettas keepers need — knowledge and commerce reinforcing each other.
