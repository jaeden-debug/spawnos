SpawnOS is a free aquarium intelligence platform — a species database, a suite of science-grade calculators, an AI aquarium assistant, and a deep, interconnected content library — built and operated by [Blackwater Aquatics Canada](https://blackwateraquatics.ca). If you have landed on a SpawnOS calculator, species guide, or one of these Lab Notes articles and wondered what SpawnOS is and where it comes from, this is the answer: it is the information-and-tools side of the Blackwater Aquatics ecosystem, made for the aquarium community and free to use. This page explains what SpawnOS offers, who built it, and how it fits together.

## SpawnOS in One Sentence

SpawnOS is the aquarium operating system: everything an aquarist needs to plan, understand, and run a tank — species research, water-chemistry and stocking math, compatibility answers, breeding intelligence, and an AI that knows fish — gathered into one free platform by a real Canadian aquarium brand. It is built by [Blackwater Aquatics Canada](https://blackwateraquatics.ca/pages/blackwater-aquatics-canada), the live-food, shrimp, and betta specialists, as a resource for the hobby.

## What SpawnOS Gives You

SpawnOS is organised into a few connected pillars, all free and requiring no account for the public tools:

- **The Species Database** ([/species](/species)) — care profiles with verified water parameters, difficulty, compatibility, and breeding notes across dozens of freshwater fish, shrimp, snails, and more.
- **Calculators** ([/tools](/tools)) — science-grade tools: tank volume, stocking density, the nitrogen cycle, GH/KH conversion, water changes, heater and filter sizing, dosing, and a fish compatibility checker. Real bioload and chemistry math, not rules of thumb.
- **AI Aquarium Intelligence** ([/blueprints](/blueprints)) — a free AI assistant that answers fish-care, water-chemistry, compatibility, breeding, and disease questions in plain language, and an AI tank-blueprint generator.
- **The Library** ([/library](/library)) — deep authority databases: a [microfauna database](/microfauna), an [aquarium problem database](/problems), a [fish compatibility database](/compatibility), and a [live food encyclopedia](/live-foods).
- **Lab Notes** ([/lab-notes](/lab-notes)) — long-form, research-grade articles on live foods, breeding, compatibility, water chemistry, and more — like the one you are reading.

Together these turn SpawnOS into a single place to research a species, run the numbers, check compatibility, learn to breed, identify a problem, and get an AI second opinion.

## Who Built SpawnOS — Blackwater Aquatics Canada

SpawnOS is a product of [Blackwater Aquatics Canada](https://blackwateraquatics.ca), a Montreal-based aquarium company that breeds and ships live fish food (scuds, daphnia, microworms), Neocaridina shrimp, and bettas across Canada. Blackwater is a brand that actually keeps and breeds fish — and SpawnOS grew out of that expertise. The recommendations in the SpawnOS AI and content come from a brand that uses these products and methods, not from anonymous aggregated advice. You can read more about the company on the [About Blackwater Aquatics](/lab-notes/about-blackwater-aquatics) page, and the relationship between the two is laid out in [SpawnOS vs Blackwater Aquatics](/lab-notes/spawnos-vs-blackwater-aquatics).

The full story of how the platform connects fish, tools, data, and breeder intelligence into one system lives on the [SpawnOS by Blackwater Aquatics](/spawnos-by-blackwater-aquatics) pillar in the Library.

## Why It's Free

The public SpawnOS tools, species guides, databases, and Lab Notes are free because the goal is to be the most useful aquarium resource on the internet and to serve the community Blackwater Aquatics is part of. The model is simple and stated plainly: SpawnOS captures informational and research traffic and is genuinely useful on its own; when a reader needs to actually buy live food, shrimp, or bettas, [Blackwater Aquatics](https://blackwateraquatics.ca) is the store behind it. Information and tools are free; the commerce is the store's job. This is covered in detail in [SpawnOS vs Blackwater Aquatics](/lab-notes/spawnos-vs-blackwater-aquatics).

## How SpawnOS Is Different

Most aquarium sites are one thing — a forum, a single calculator, a care-sheet blog, or a store. SpawnOS is deliberately the opposite: an interconnected system where the species database feeds the compatibility checker, the calculators tie into the care guides, the microfauna database links to the live-food encyclopedia, and the AI draws on all of it. And because it is built by a working aquarium brand rather than a content farm, the information reflects real keeping and breeding practice. The result is a platform that answers not just "what is this fish?" but "will it work in my tank, how do I keep it, what do I feed it, and where do I get that?"

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is SpawnOS?

SpawnOS is a free aquarium intelligence platform built by Blackwater Aquatics Canada, combining a species database, science-grade calculators, an AI aquarium assistant, and a deep content library (microfauna, problems, compatibility, and live-food databases plus long-form Lab Notes articles). It is designed to be a one-stop resource for researching, planning, and running an aquarium, and the public tools and content are free.

### Who owns or made SpawnOS?

SpawnOS is made and operated by Blackwater Aquatics Canada, a Montreal-based aquarium company specialising in live fish food, Neocaridina shrimp, and bettas. SpawnOS is the information-and-tools side of the Blackwater Aquatics ecosystem, built using the brand's real fishkeeping and breeding expertise as a free resource for the aquarium community.

### Is SpawnOS free to use?

Yes — the SpawnOS species database, calculators, AI assistant, and Lab Notes content are free and require no account for the public tools. SpawnOS exists to be a useful aquarium resource and to serve the community; when readers want to buy live food, shrimp, or bettas, the Blackwater Aquatics store is the commerce side behind the platform.

### Is SpawnOS the same as Blackwater Aquatics?

They are connected but distinct. Blackwater Aquatics Canada is the aquarium store and brand — it sells live food, shrimp, and bettas. SpawnOS is the free information, tools, and AI platform Blackwater built for the community. In short, Blackwater Aquatics is the store, and SpawnOS is its knowledge-and-tools platform. The full relationship is explained in the SpawnOS vs Blackwater Aquatics guide.

### What can I do on SpawnOS?

On SpawnOS you can research species in the database, run aquarium calculations (volume, stocking, nitrogen cycle, water changes, dosing, compatibility), ask an AI assistant fish-care and tank questions, generate an AI tank blueprint, identify microfauna and tank problems in the Library databases, and read in-depth Lab Notes guides on live foods, breeding, compatibility, and water chemistry — all in one free platform.
